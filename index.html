AMazing

AMazing is a maze escaping game, the player is control himself to find the way and escaped from the maze. There will be some trophies in the maze that player can choose to collect them, the trophies will be hind in different places in the maze. Every trophies collected, the palyer will have the visial for the whole map for seconds. There are also few ghosts hiding in the maze, when player close to the ghosts, ghosts will be alarmed, and start the chase mode and chasing the player, once the player been catches, the game will restart. The goal of the game is finding out the way to escape from the maze, try not to be been caught by the ghosts, and collect the trophies as much as possible.

The game AI in this game will be the ghosts. The ghosts will hide in the dark and once they target the player nearby, they will speed up, if player can go outside of the area that ghosts can target, they will move back to the original place. The motion planning of the ghosts: looking for player nearby with a fix area that they can target, once they target the player, they will move straight to the player, ghosts cannot move across the wall just like the player. There will be collision detect between wall, ghosts, player and the trophies The procedural content generation will have a map for different levels, which contain the start point of the player, the exit of the maze, the ghosts at different place and the trophies in different corner
